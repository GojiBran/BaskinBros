Goji's Meals

Deli Sandwiches

Ingredients: Requested bread, meat, cheese, veggies, etc.

Preparation: Assemble ingredients into a sandwich.

Chicken Nugget Wraps

Ingredients: Tortilla, lettuce, cheese, sliced nuggets, seasonings, Hidden Valley Ranch

Preparation: Roll ingredients in a tortilla for a quick wrap.

Fried Potatoes with Egg and Toast

Ingredients: Potatoes, eggs, bread, butter, seasoning

Preparation: Dice and fry potatoes, cook eggs as desired, toast bread with butter.

Instant Ramen

Ingredients: Instant ramen (preferably Shin Ramyun Black), optional add-ins (protein, egg, green onion, etc.)

Preparation: Boil water, cook ramen, add seasonings, plate, add toppings.

Chili Dogs

Ingredients: Hot dog bun, hot dog, canned chili beans (Dennison), cheese (optional), onions (optional)

Preparation: Cook hot dog (toast bun if desired), place dog in bun, top with chili, cheese, and onions.

Chili Bean Taco Salad

Ingredients: Nacho cheese Doritos, lettuce, cheese, Hidden Valley Ranch, canned chili beans (Dennison)

Preparation: Layer ingredients in a bowl, top with ranch dressing.

Chili Bean Burritos

Ingredients: Tortilla, canned chili beans (Dennison), cheese, Hidden Valley Ranch, nacho cheese Doritos

Preparation: Heat tortilla, fill with heated chili beans, cheese, and optional toppings like Hidden Valley Ranch or crushed nacho cheese Doritos.

Quesadillas

Ingredients: Tortillas, cheese, seasoning, hot sauce, requested toppings

Preparation: Heat tortillas with cheese, seasoning, and toppings in a pan with oil.

Kraft Mac & Cheese

Ingredients: Kraft mac & cheese, hot dog (optional), canned chili beans (Dennison, optional)

Preparation: Prepare mac & cheese, add hot dog and/or chili beans for extra flavor.

Hamburger Helper

Ingredients: Stroganoff, cheeseburger mac, etc.

Preparation: Follow instructions on the Hamburger Helper box.
